Technical Roles Software Engineer/Developer: Designs, develops, tests, and maintains software applications or systems. Backend Developer: Focuses on server-side development, databases, and application logic. Frontend Developer: Works on the user interface and user experience of applications. Full-Stack Developer: Handles both frontend and backend development tasks. DevOps Engineer: Bridges the gap between development and operations, focusing on deployment, automation, and infrastructure. Quality Assurance (QA) Engineer: Tests software to find bugs and ensure it meets quality standards. Data Scientist/Analyst: Analyzes data to help make informed business decisions and improve products. UX/UI Designer: Designs user interfaces and ensures a positive user experience. Security Engineer: Protects software and systems from security threats and vulnerabilities. Product and Project Management Product Manager: Defines product vision, strategy, and roadmap. Works closely with development teams and stakeholders. Project Manager: Oversees project timelines, resources, and deliverables, ensuring projects are completed on time and within scope. Customer and Support Roles Customer Support Specialist: Assists users with issues, troubleshooting, and inquiries. Technical Support Engineer: Provides advanced technical assistance and resolves complex issues. Business and Operations Business Analyst: Gathers and analyzes business requirements and translates them into technical specifications.Sales Engineer: Works with the sales team to understand customer needs and provide technical solutions. Marketing Specialist: Promotes the company’s products and services through various marketing strategies. HR Specialist/Recruiter: Handles hiring, employee relations, and organizational development. Leadership and Strategy Chief Technology Officer (CTO): Oversees the company’s technology strategy and development.
